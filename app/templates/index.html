Bellybutton Biodiversity:

This Dashboard visualizes data for a specific sample in the belly button biodiversity study found on robdunnlab.com.

Visuals Include:

  • Sample Metadata: With information about the individual which the sample was collected
  • Pie Chart: Showing up to the top 10 bacteria found in the selected samples belly button
  • Weekly Washing Gauge: Showing the weekly washing frequency of the selected sample
  • Bubble Chart: Showing all of the bacteria found with marker size proportional to the value of the Operational Taxonomic Unit found in the sample
